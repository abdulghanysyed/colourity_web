Features

Colour-First. Style Always.

From personal colour analysis to what to wear today — Colourity is the only app that starts with you, not just your wardrobe.

Personal Colour Analysis

Ever wondered which colour suits me? Scan your face and Colourity's AI reveals your seasonal colour palette and undertone in seconds — warm, cool, or neutral.

AI-Powered Wardrobe

Photograph your clothes and Colourity identifies colour, pattern, and style automatically — no manual tagging, no effort. Your entire wardrobe, digitised.

Smart Outfit Matching

Stop guessing what to wear. Get outfit combinations from your own wardrobe, ranked by real colour-harmony rules — not generic suggestions.

Shop the Gap

Missing the right piece? Get shopping recommendations matched to your exact colours — linked to Myntra, Amazon, and Flipkart at launch.

Occasion-Based Styling

Tell us where you're going — interview, wedding, work, casual — and get the right outfit for the moment, already matched to your personal colours.

Weather-Aware Recommendations

Colourity checks the weather so your outfit is both colour-correct and season-appropriate — no more overdressing or underdressing.

Mix-and-Match Generation

Uncover combinations you never considered. Colourity's AI scours your entire wardrobe for colour-harmonious pairings hiding in plain sight.

Style Insights & Analytics

See which colours dominate your wardrobe, which pieces never get worn, and get personalised tips to refresh your look without buying more.

Secure Cloud Sync

Your colour profile and digital wardrobe are safely backed up and synced across all your devices. Your style travels with you, everywhere.

How It Works

01

Discover Your Colour Palette
