Features

Everything Your Wardrobe Needs

Nine powerful AI-driven features designed to transform how you dress — every single day.

AI Garment Recognition

Simply photograph any clothing item. Our AI instantly identifies the type, color, pattern, and style — no manual tagging needed.

Automatic Wardrobe Organization

Your clothes are automatically sorted by category, color, season, and occasion — so you always know what you own.

Intelligent Color Matching

Get outfit recommendations based on color theory and your personal style — tops, bottoms, shoes, and accessories, all perfectly matched.

Occasion-Based Suggestions

Tell Colourity where you're headed — Work, Casual, Party, Travel, Date Night — and get outfits perfectly suited to the occasion.

Weather-Aware Recommendations

Colourity checks the weather and ensures your outfit is not just stylish but appropriate for the day's conditions.

Mix-and-Match Generation

Discover unexpected outfit combinations you never thought of. Colourity's AI explores your entire wardrobe to find hidden gems.

Wishlist & Shopping Integration

Identify gaps in your wardrobe and get suggestions for pieces that will complement what you already own. Smart shopping, simplified.

Style Insights & Analytics

Discover your personal style patterns — which colors you wear most, underused items, and personalized tips to refresh your look.

Secure Cloud Sync

Your digital wardrobe is safely backed up and synced across all your devices. Your style travels with you, everywhere.
